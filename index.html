Lowe's Delievery Service

Additional information

If you choose shipping as your delivery method, your order will be delivered by a third-party shipping provider: UPS, FedEx or the US Postal Service. These carriers offer order tracking service, which allows you to monitor your package as it makes its way to a final address.

When your order has been shipped, you'll receive an email shipping confirmation containing a tracking or delivery confirmation number. To check the status of your shipment:

• Follow the instructions included in your shipping confirmation email to track your order through one of our carriers' websites.

• Check your order status online.

• Call Lowe's customer care at 1-800-445-6937 Monday through Saturday, 7 a.m. to 1 a.m. (ET) or Sunday, 8 a.m. to 9 p.m. (ET).

• Email us at customercare@lowes.com for a status update.

With a MyLowe's account, you can track your entire purchase history. Learn more by exploring MyLowe's.

If you don't have a MyLowe's account, we can locate and provide a receipt for purchases made via credit, debit and check within 90 days. For purchases made with a Lowe's Credit Card, Business Account or Accounts Receivable, you can view your purchases online by logging into your credit account here.

You can request a receipt for purchases made within 90 days by calling Lowe's customer care at 1-800-445-6937.
